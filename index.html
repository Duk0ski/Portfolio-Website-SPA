About Me

I’m Daniel Dukoski, a motivated and adaptable full-stack web developer from Gostivar, with a strong foundation in programming, problem-solving, and collaboration. My passion for coding began at a young age when I started building simple websites using HTML and CSS, learning from YouTube tutorials.

In high school, I expanded my knowledge with JavaScript, strengthening my frontend development skills, and later delved deeper into C++, refining my problem-solving abilities.

Currently, I’m a student at FINKI, continuously expanding my expertise. My skills include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and experience with responsive design, Bootstrap, and React.js. On the backend, I have a basic understanding of server-side development and MySQL, with a strong desire to further develop in this area. Additionally, I’m proficient in Git, GitHub, Docker, and other development tools that enhance efficiency and collaboration.

With a commitment to continuous growth and innovation, I thrive in team-oriented environments and enjoy tackling challenges that push the boundaries of technology. I’m always eager to contribute to impactful projects and bring ideas to life.
